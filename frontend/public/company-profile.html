About Us

Who We Are

Qloud Tech, a division of Qloud Smart Homes, is a premier smart home automation and home theatre solutions provider based in Karnataka, India.

Founded in 2020 by MA Haldipur, we have rapidly grown to become one of the most trusted names in the home automation industry.

With over 8 years of industry experience and a passionate team of professionals, we have successfully transformed 200+ homes into intelligent living spaces that combine cutting-edge technology with elegant design.

"To revolutionize the way people experience their homes by making smart living accessible, affordable, and seamless for every household in India."

We envision a future where every home is a smart home — where technology works silently in the background to enhance comfort, security, and entertainment.
